Elevate

This is the DV program we talked about that we have produced a draft program for. Depending on COVID-19 restrictions, we may or may not be able to run trials of this program before the end of the year. We would like two types of information about ELEVATE on the website, firstly information about the program itself that teachers and other stakeholders can look at. Secondly, we would like information that students can access for information about recognising and getting help for various DV situations. It would include links to various other agencies that can help also. Kind of a ‘one stop shop’ for young people with questions about Domestic and Family Violence. The ELEVATE program will be delivered by serving police officers, with help from teachers. Each program will consist of 6 lessons spread over 3 weeks, so 2 lessons per week of approx. 1 – ½ to 2 hours long. We will be evaluating this program just like we evaluate our EDGE program.
