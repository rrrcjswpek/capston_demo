The success of our EDGE program has exceeded our expectations, and the robust evaluation completed for us by KPMG confirms the positive
outcomes. We are learning as we go, and have already made minor adjustments that will take effect in the programs to come. We are gearing up to
deliver more programs in these areas with more locations added each term. With such a diverse range of locations it is very important that the program
remains flexible, in order to make best use of local resources, both human and otherwise!
